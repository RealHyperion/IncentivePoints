IncentivePoints Payment Plans

Standard Plan ($1/month)

  • Earn points at the base rate.
  • Cash out at **100 points**.
  • Milestone Rewards:
    • 10 Points: Get a small snack (like candy or chips).
    • 25 Points: Choose a treat up to $5 (like ice cream or a drink).
    • 50 Points: Receive a small gift or surprise item (up to $10 value).
    • 75 Points: Bonus +5 points and priority for future rewards.
    • 100 Points: Cash out $100 or opt for a larger reward (e.g., event tickets, merch).
  • Community Engagement: Participate in regular group chats for encouragement and fun challenges.

Premium Plan ($5/month)

  • Earn points at a **10% increased rate** compared to Standard.
  • Cash out at **50 points**.
  • Milestone Rewards:
    • 10 Points: Get your favorite snack (up to $5).
    • 20 Points: Lunch reward (up to $10).
    • 40 Points: Surprise gift or premium item (up to $15 value).
    • 60 Points: Bonus +10 points and early access to special events.
    • 75 Points: Option to cash out $25 or choose a premium experience.
    • 100 Points: Cash out $100, plus receive a **Premium Member badge** and a small personalized gift.
  • Exclusive Access: Private challenges and invitations to exclusive events (like movie nights).

VIP Plan ($10/month)

  • Earn points at a **20% increased rate** compared to Standard.
  • Cash out at **25 points**.
  • Milestone Rewards:
    • 5 Points: Get a snack (up to $5) of your choice.
    • 15 Points: Treat yourself to lunch or coffee (up to $10).
    • 30 Points: Larger gift or special experience (up to $20 value).
    • 50 Points: Bonus +15 points and a special VIP gift.
    • 75 Points: Special recognition and access to VIP-only events.
    • 100 Points: Cash out $100, plus receive a **$25 gift card** for a local restaurant or online shopping.
  • VIP Perks: Access to a private chat for VIPs, monthly challenges, and exclusive invitations to special events.
