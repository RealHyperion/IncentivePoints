IncentivePoints Info

How to Earn Points

  • Helping with Tasks (+2 IP): Assist with anything I ask, such as carrying items, helping with projects, or offering support in any personal task.
  • Respectful Communication (+1 IP): Speak politely and respectfully at all times, whether in person, over messages, or in group settings.
  • Positive Feedback (+2 IP): Provide constructive and encouraging feedback about ideas, projects, or decisions I make.
  • Following My Lead (+2 IP): Show respect for my leadership in group activities or personal decisions by being cooperative and following instructions.
  • Surprising Me with Help or Support (+5 IP): Go out of your way to help or offer unexpected support without me having to ask.
  • Doing Something Kind for Me (+3 IP): Surprise me with a kind gesture like offering to help out when I’m stressed or taking on something I care about.
  • Volunteering for Tasks (+2 IP): Proactively offer to help with something before I ask for help.

How to Lose Points

  • Disrespecting Me (-5 IP): If you speak rudely, mock, or behave disrespectfully toward me, you will lose points immediately.
  • Ignoring Me (-4 IP): If I ask for help or try to talk to you, and you completely ignore me or act dismissively.
  • Not Keeping Your Word (-3 IP): If you make a promise or commit to something and don’t follow through without a good reason.
  • Publicly Disrespecting Me (-6 IP): Disrespecting or embarrassing me in front of others will result in losing more points.
  • Talking Behind My Back (-8 IP): If I find out you've been talking negatively about me to others, this will be an immediate point deduction.
  • Being Uncooperative (-4 IP): Refusing to help or being difficult to work with when I request your cooperation.
  • Spreading Rumors or Lies About Me (-10 IP): Lying or spreading false information about me will result in a significant loss of points.
  • Undermining My Decisions (-5 IP): If you constantly challenge or question my decisions in a disrespectful manner, you will lose points.

Overall Rules

  • The maximum amount of points you can earn is **100 IP**.
  • Points will be reviewed and adjusted weekly.
  • Points can be redeemed for rewards, such as snacks, lunch, or exclusive events.
  • The leaderboard will be updated monthly to showcase top earners.
